Casa Vista Huanacaxtle

Perched high on the hillside, this modern home overlooks the quaint fishing town of La Cruz and out onto the Bay of Banderas.Wake up to stunning morning views of the town’s tropical coastal setting. At night, enjoy the lights of the “big city” of Puerto Vallarta in the distance.

The space

The house offers perfect spaces and amenities for both individual privacy and large gatherings.

This modern home is designed in a minimalist architectural style with open spaces and large crystal doors to take advantage of the stunning views. The living area, kitchen, pool, and all 3 bedrooms are situated to include the home’s gorgeous view. Each bedroom has a private bath and balcony, the large rooftop deck is outfitted for everything needed to host gatherings, and the closed garage can accommodate 2 vehicles.
