Super Markets

For a more serious groceries run you might want to go to the super market, there a 3 located in Bucerias about 15min heading towards Puerto Vallarta

  1. Chedraui
    Chedraui is the closest to the house, and has most of the things you will need.
  2. Mega Comercial Mexicana
    Is a little bit further down the road, my wife likes this one best, it has a good balance of price and quality and you can get some imported items that maybe you just cant leave without.
  3. La Comer
    Is right next to the Mega (i know leave it to the mexicans to open a super market right in front of another one). This is the newest and biggest out of all 3, the prices are a little bit higher but stock higher end products too.
